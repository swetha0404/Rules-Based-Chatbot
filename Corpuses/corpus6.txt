Sheila Johnston mentions that Keanu appeared in the Leah Poslun’s Theatre School production of   For Adults Only  in the first chapter of her book ,  Keanu. At the time of his tenure, Rose Dubin was the director of the school. Rose Dubin has posted photos on her  website  from some of the school’s performances including a photo of the playbill for  For Adults Only  as well as two photos featuring Keanu in  Romeo & Juliet   (he played Mercutio). Keanu’s performance in  For Adults Only  led talent agent Tracy Moore, who was in the audience for one of the shows, to sign him. In the book, Ms. Moore says:  ‘I sent him out on a lot of casting calls for commercials, just to get him started. And every time he went out he seemed to get something. He was a sure sell in a way. He was always extremely intense – he took his work very seriously.
